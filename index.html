how we got here

I grew up with early access to a computer becoming obsessed with making content I learned photoshop by the time i was 9 and by 13 getting deep in blender for 3D design doing thumbnails for some of the biggest Fortnite YouTubers. Eventually i got into ecomerce starting my own clothing brand Minimaru at 14 which scaled past $1M in revenue within a year and a half through viral marketing. By 17 I was burnt out with my business and began learning to code starting a club at my school with my best friend documenting the journey on Serenidad


After graduating early highschool I joined Hack Club, growing their Instagram to 100K followers. At 19 I moved to rural Japan for 2 months building an anime storyboarding tool for studios. After I got into 3D printing and CAD eventually joining Nautilus, a 3 month residency in San Francisco where I built a drone and walked from SF to Palo Alto in a day.


I left marketing and software behind, went deep into neuroscience, joining a biotech stealth start up doing research on brain ispc and working on ideas to help map the brain
