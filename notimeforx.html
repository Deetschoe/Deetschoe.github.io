Privacy Policy for No Time for X

Effective Date: October 22, 2024

Thank you for using No Time for X. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data while using our Chrome extension.

1. Information We Collect

No Time for X collects minimal information necessary to provide the core functionality of redirecting users based on browsing behavior. The data collected includes:

  • Web History: The extension collects and stores data about the number of times you visit certain websites based on your specified conditions. This information is only used to determine when to trigger the redirection rules you've set.

No Time for X does not collect any personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or location, nor do we collect sensitive information such as financial or health data.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To monitor the number of times specific websites are accessed.
  • To trigger the redirection of websites based on user-defined conditions.

3. Data Storage

Your preferences and web history data are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data is not transferred or transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

4. Third-Party Sharing

No Time for X does not sell, share, or otherwise disclose your information to third parties. The extension operates entirely on your device and does not communicate with any external services or databases.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All data collected by the extension is stored locally and is not shared with any external servers. However, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data in the event of unforeseen security vulnerabilities in Chrome or your device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The most current version will always be available on our extension's Chrome Web Store page.

7. Your Consent

By using No Time for X, you consent to our collection and use of your data as outlined in this privacy policy.
